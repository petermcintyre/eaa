Our Values

We’re trying to change the world for the better. Utilising world-class research, we use our donations to support the most effective methods of changing lives.

COMPASSION

We value all lives, no matter when or where they’re born.

OPEN-MINDED

We consider all causes and actions, and then act in the way that will help those in need the most.

FOCUS ON RESULTS

We focus on results, because they matter. We apply evidence and reason to determine the most effective ways to improve the world.

What We Do

  • 01.

    Partnership

    We partner with the most effective organisations we can find to change lives.

  • 02.

    Research

    We use research from GiveWell, The Open Philanthropy Project, Giving What We Can, The Global Priorities Project and academia to direct funding to the best non-profits we can find.

  • 03.

    Funding

    We are open to funding any organisation that can demonstrate how it is effectively helping those in need. Apply to us here.

Our Values

Effective Altruism Australia is a tax deductible foundation that make grants to support the charities that are most effective at saving lives. We focus on supporting the charities that are ranked highly by charity evaluators.

What We Do

    Partnership

    We partner with the most effective organisations we can find to change lives.

  • 02.

    Research

    We use research from GiveWell, The Open Philanthropy Project, Giving What We Can, The Global Priorities Project and academia to direct funding to the best non-profits we can find.
